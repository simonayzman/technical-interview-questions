### Minimum Maze Path

Given an m x n matrix of integers, starting from the upper left hand corner and moving only down or right, find the minimum possible value of a path to get to the bottom right hand corner. For example,

|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | 8 | 5 | 1 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 7 | 5 | 8 | 9 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 3 | 6 | 5 | 9 | 3 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | 1 | 6 | 8 | 0 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
